# Decision Log

Use this file to record project decisions that affect contributors, maintainers, users, or the long-term direction of the project.

## Decision Template

### YYYY-MM-DD: Decision Title

- Status: proposed | accepted | superseded
- Owners: maintainer names or team
- Context: what problem or constraint led to the decision
- Decision: what the project is doing
- Alternatives considered: options that were discussed and why they were not chosen
- Contributor impact: how this affects issues, pull requests, support, or documentation
- Review date: when maintainers should revisit the decision

## Decisions

### 2026-01-01: Example Decision

- Status: accepted
- Owners: Maintainer Team
- Context: Contributors needed a clear place to find project-level decisions.
- Decision: Maintain a lightweight decision log in the repository.
- Alternatives considered: Use only issue discussions, but important decisions become difficult to find.
- Contributor impact: Contributors can understand why maintainers chose a direction before opening related work.
- Review date: 2026-04-01
